Invitation

Dear invitee,

We want to invite you to participate in a 3-day, invite-only, interactive workshop on advanced data sketching techniques.
The goals of this workshop are:

  • Bridging the gap between academia and participants, identifying technical and non-technical roadblocks to wider adoption of sketch techniques.
  • Identifying and developing a common vision on the key open problems in our community.
  • Sustain existing and ofstering new collaborations among academics and practitioners from industry or research institutes.

Look below for the full program and logistical information, as well as the motivation behind these goals.
Please indicate your attendance in the form below.

We hope to see you there!
Minos Garofalakis, Odysseas Papapetrou, Wieger Punter
